The Eldoret National Polytechnic-Town Campus (Sports Registration Database System)

This is a project that I developed within three hours. I did this during the registration
of students who were likely to participate in the up-coming sports events then.
Courtesy of critical thinking soft skills, I came up with the Platform after a short notice to ease information sharing between the Settlite campus officials and registration of students in the seven category of events within the shortest duration.

The registered athletes on the inter-departmental competition events captured the students and assigned them them the activities they will be undertaking. The captured data helped and ease the planning by 60%. The Platform was also intended to keep the records and digitize the entire process of student registrations and stored in
an Excel database for easier and future retrieval
